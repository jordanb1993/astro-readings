Two planets, same natal point, completely different questions. The Node asked whether the path runs through natal Jupiter in the Scorpio 5th, and it confirmed yes, at 0.02° yesterday, now 0.05° past and moving on. Uranus is the approaching planet now, 0.21° out and tightening toward June 14–15 exact. The Node brought recognition; Uranus brings disruption. The Scorpio 5th is the place where the creative work is either fully inhabited or it isn't. Recognition of that is one thing. What Uranus is building toward is a structural question: something in how the work is configured needs to shift before it can expand in the direction the work is pointing. The inconjunct doesn't offer a clean line of sight. It requires finding the new angle by adjustment, not by calculation.

Jupiter is simultaneously inconjunct the Lot of Fortune in the 9th at 0.13° applying, exact tomorrow. The 9th is belief structures: what you think opportunity looks like, what frameworks you use to recognize it when it arrives. An inconjunct from Jupiter doesn't simply expand the fortune; it asks for a recalibration of the entry angle. The opportunity may be present and arriving in a form that doesn't fit the existing framework. The adjustment needed is small and specific, not a renovation of the whole worldview.

Pluto retrograde is at 0.26° from natal Moon in the 10th, tightening steadily toward late June. In the 10th, where career and emotional life run on the same circuit, Pluto retrograde presses inward: not an external restructuring but a compression of what's authentic against what's been performed. The authority being built here is interior before it's visible.

> *The trine confirmed the direction. The inconjunct is asking what form the direction takes from here.*
