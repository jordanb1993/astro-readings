# 🌑 Taurus New Moon Ritual
## May 16, 2026 · Exact at 3:01 PM Chicago

---

> *Taurus asks one question above all others: what do you actually want to build? Not perform. Not manage. Build — with your hands, your body, your values, your time. This New Moon is your answer.*

---

## Why This New Moon Is Different

This isn't a soft, pink-candle, intentions-in-a-journal New Moon. The seed falls at **~26° Taurus** — nearly exactly opposite your natal Pluto at 25° Scorpio in your 6th house. Pluto oppositions from a New Moon mean whatever gets planted here has roots that go deep and stay. The 6th house is your daily life: your craft, your work, your body, your service. This New Moon is seeding transformation in the texture of how you actually live.

It also lands the morning after **Uranus crossed exact opposition to your natal Sun** for the first time — the identity renovation's first pass. You're not the same person who set intentions at the last New Moon. The container changed overnight. What goes into the ground today grows in the renovated version of you, not the old one.

**Timing:** The ritual can happen anytime on May 16. Close to 3:01 PM is potent. The evening is also beautiful — after the exact peak, in the first hours of the new cycle. Up to 24 hours after is still in the New Moon window.

## What You'll Need

You likely already have most of this.

- **Sage or palo santo** — for clearing
- **Small bowl of salt water** — your established cleansing practice
- **2–3 bay leaves** — for the release burn
- **A green candle** — Taurus/Venus color, for what's being planted
- **A white candle** — for the self, clarity, the renovated identity
- **Rosemary** — memory of power (you used this in the April ritual; it's right)
- **Something earthy** — a plant you own, a handful of soil if you have it, a crystal (rose quartz, pyrite, anything green or golden). If you have nothing like this, your bare feet on the floor will do it.
- **Journal or nice paper** — for the written intentions; these are meant to be kept, not burned
- **Something for the close** — a specific playlist, a bath, a meal you love. More on this below.

## The Ritual

### 1. Clear and Ground *(5 minutes)*

Sage the space — corners, doorways, yourself. Take your shoes off. Stand still for a moment and feel the floor under your feet. This sounds simple; for Taurus it's the whole point. You are a body on the earth. Let that be true for a minute before anything else.

If you have a plant in the apartment, put your hands on it or near it. Earth, even through a pot.

---

### 2. Salt Water Cleansing *(your established practice)*

Forehead — clear what you've been carrying mentally through the Balsamic phase.
Throat — this is where the **Queen of Swords** lives. What gets said on that call with Eleanor. What you say to yourself when the Chiron loop activates.
Heart — the porous Neptune-Moon field you've been living in. Honor it; you don't need to close it.
Hands — the craft. The work. What gets built.

---

### 3. The Release *(brief — this isn't the main event tonight)*

The Balsamic phase already did most of the releasing work. You don't need a major cord-cutting ritual here — the tarot reading, the recognition, the clarity have already moved through you. This is just the formal close.

**Write on a bay leaf (or two):**

One: *The pattern Eleanor mirrored — the maternal echo, the pull toward familiar over nourishing.*
Two: *The "not enough" loop traveling with me into the new cycle.*

Burn them. The Balsamic phase ends. The new one starts.

---

### 4. Light the Candles

Green first — for what's being planted, for the body, for what endures.
White second — for the self in renovation, the identity that just had its first Uranus pass.

Hold the rosemary. This is memory of power — the reminder that you have been capable, have delivered, have held your ground. You carried it through the Tony spiral and the April confrontation. It belongs here too.

---

### 5. The Four Seeds *(the heart of the ritual)*

Write these on paper you'll keep. Take your time — Taurus hates rushing and so does anything real.

---

**🌱 Seed One — The Renovated Self**

Uranus crossed exact opposition to your natal Sun last night. The identity renovation isn't finished — it's between passes — but the first threshold is behind you. Before the second pass comes with more data, plant something here.

*Write: One true thing about who I'm becoming that wasn't fully available before.*

Not a goal. Not a task. A true thing. It might be about how you want to be seen at work. It might be about what you're done pretending to be fine with. It might be about the version of yourself that doesn't perform ease she doesn't feel. Write whatever arrives without managing it.

---

**🌱 Seed Two — The Two of Cups Frequency**

The deck left you the Two of Cups at the very bottom last night. Not "send me someone." A tuning fork — what genuine mutuality actually feels and sounds like, so you know it when it arrives.

*Write: What genuine reciprocity feels like in my body when it's actually present.*

Specific. Not "I want someone who texts back." What's the felt quality? What does it feel like to be actually met? Go there — even if it's something you've only had in small doses. The body knows. The Taurus New Moon will hold it.

---

**🌱 Seed Three — Financial Roots**

Taurus is the earth sign of resources, stability, and what endures. You have more momentum here than you've had in years: the garnishment cleared, first full paycheck, savings at $2,384, the rehab started, the credit score climbing. This New Moon is made for planting financial intention.

*Write: One specific, concrete financial root I'm planting in this cycle.*

It might be: *By the Taurus Full Moon in November, I have mailed the rehabilitation form and received confirmation.* Or: *I have a dedicated Ireland fund and it has a number in it.* Or: *I know my credit score monthly.* Pick the one that's most alive. One seed, planted clearly, is more powerful than a list.

---

**🌱 Seed Four — The Body**

Four-plus weeks on the walking pad. Arms Tuesday and Thursday. Squats Wednesday and Saturday. Your back pain is reduced. Your energy is better than it's been in years. Your body is doing something right now, and Taurus New Moon is asking you to honor that and name the next layer.

*Write: What my body is asking for next — not a punishment, a pleasure.*

Could be: more sleep. A specific food you've been craving. Dancing. A massage. Longer walks. The Ireland trip as a physical goal — your feet on that coast. Listen to what the body says when the Virgo Moon isn't immediately editing it.

---

**The Pluto Note**

Because this New Moon sits opposite your natal Pluto in the 6th house, there's one more thing to name — not a fifth seed, just a thread woven through all four. Pluto in the 6th is transformation in daily life and craft. The question underneath all of it:

*After the renovation, what does the daily life I'm actually building look like?*

You don't need to answer it fully tonight. Just let it be present.

---

### 6. Hold and Speak *(3–5 minutes)*

Read the four seeds aloud with the rosemary in your hand. Not for performance — just so your voice carries them, so the throat gets to be part of it alongside the hand that wrote them. The Queen of Swords speaks. Let these intentions be spoken.

Place the paper somewhere intentional — a drawer, a book, somewhere it won't be accidentally thrown away. You'll want to read it at the Taurus Full Moon in November.

---

### 7. Close With Pleasure *(this is the most important part)*

**In Taurus, pleasure IS the prayer.** You don't close this ritual with more processing. You close it by feeding the body something it loves. This is the seal on the intention — your nervous system registering that abundance is available, that good things are physical and real, that you are here and alive and deserve to enjoy that.

Pick one (or all three if you want to make an evening of it):

🎵 **A playlist** — music you love, not background music. Something that means something. Let it be the soundtrack of the new cycle opening. You know which one.

🛁 **A bath** — sea salt, your favorite scent. Slow. No phone. Just the body in warm water knowing it did something tonight.

🍽️ **A meal you genuinely love** — not "what's in the fridge." The thing you actually want. Eaten slowly, with presence. Taurus asks: can you receive something good without immediately doing something else?

The intention is sealed when the body is satisfied. That's Taurus. That's the ritual.
