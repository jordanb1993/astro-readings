# Readings Archive
*Dated transit readings — one file per session*
*Format: YYYY-MM-DD.md*

---

## Purpose

A searchable record of actual readings so patterns can be tracked across months — not just the correlations that made it into KNOWLEDGE.md, but the full text of what was said and what was happening. Lets us look back at what I predicted vs. what actually unfolded.

---

## File Naming

`YYYY-MM-DD.md` — e.g., `2026-04-21.md`

For multiple readings on the same day: `2026-04-21-evening.md`

---

## What to Include

- Transiting planets and degrees used (from Time Passages or typed)
- Full reading text
- Any follow-up reflection Jordan added after the fact
- Verified correlations to note in KNOWLEDGE.md later
