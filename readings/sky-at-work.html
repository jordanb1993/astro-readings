💬 FOR COMMUNICATION

Mercury is in Gemini (its home sign) sitting in a sextile to Saturn in Aries, exact midweek. That combination is unusually good for anything that needs to move quickly and hold up under scrutiny: proposals, written follow-ups, contracts, documentation. The precision register is open and available.

The key move this week: write it down. Decisions made in conversation but not recorded are likely to get revisited under this sky. Anything verbally agreed in the last two weeks but not formalized — this is the week to close that loop. Verbal alignment alone won't hold when Saturn is watching.

Best days: Tuesday–Wednesday. Avoid loose async on Monday when the holiday rhythm is still diffusing.

🔥 FOR DECISIONS & MOMENTUM

Mars in Taurus squaring Pluto Rx in Aquarius is the dominant texture all week. This doesn't stop things from moving — it means whatever you push on will push back, and the resistance tends to be structural rather than personal. That's useful data: if something keeps meeting friction, the question is whether you're pressing against the wrong constraint, or the right one.

Venus sextile Mars is still in orb from last week, separating but present through Wednesday. The collaborative ask and the action impulse are still reasonably aligned through midweek — if something needs both relational goodwill and forward momentum, earlier in the week carries more fuel.

Best days for forward moves: Tuesday–Wednesday. Thursday, when Venus-Saturn tightens, the "not yet" register gets louder.

✦ ♀ ✦
♀ FOR COLLABORATION

Venus at 7° Cancer is squaring both Neptune and Saturn this week — the relational field has a "prove it" quality. Warmth reads as genuine but also gets interrogated. Collaborative pitches built on actual substance land; anything relying primarily on enthusiasm or relationship-credit as its argument will get examined more carefully than it would in a softer week.

This isn't a bad week for collaboration — it's a demanding week. The people who've done the work hold the room. The background note: Saturn conjunct Neptune in Aries (ongoing all of 2026) keeps asking what the concrete plan is behind the idea. Teams that can answer that feel the pressure as useful.
