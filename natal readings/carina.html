Leo 9th House Visibility Intensity

Mars at 22° Leo and Chiron at 19° Leo sit 3° apart in your 9th house. Drive and wound share a sign, a house, and a decade of degree.

Every time you act with full Leo boldness, the part of you that fears not being seen activates at the same moment. The wound doesn't stop the drive. But it colors it.

The tight square to Pluto (0.44°) adds intensity that doesn't release easily. When you assert a belief, take a creative risk, or step into visibility, the interior weight of that action feels far larger than what's legible from the outside. Pluto in Scorpio in the 12th doesn't do surface-level; even partially below your own visibility, something is registering this as significant.

This also builds extraordinary staying power. Once committed, you don't quit. The wound is the price of that power. And the power is real.

The wound doesn't make the fire smaller. It makes every flame feel like it has to prove something. It doesn't.
