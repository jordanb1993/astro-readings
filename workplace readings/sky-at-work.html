For Communication

Mercury moves into Cancer today. One-on-ones will go better than group settings this week. People open up more in direct conversation. Group meetings tend to stay surface-level.

Mercury in Gemini was fast and information-first. Cancer Mercury is different: it cares more about what matters than what is accurate. In practice, one-on-ones go better than group meetings, a personal touch opens more doors than a polished argument, and people say more of what they actually think.

The catch: Mercury squares Neptune mid-week (peaks Tuesday through Wednesday). This combination produces communication that sounds right but has not been pressure-tested. You will feel confident—check the underlying logic before anything goes external. Write it Tuesday. Send it Wednesday.
