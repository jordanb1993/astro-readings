## What This Is

Your natal North Node is at approximately 3°02' Sagittarius in the 6th house. Your natal South Node is at 3°02' Gemini in the 12th house. The nodal axis represents the soul's trajectory in this lifetime: the South Node is the accumulated past-life and early-life pattern — what comes instinctively, what you return to under pressure, what the soul knows so well it can do it without thinking. The North Node is the growth direction — less instinctive, requiring intentional choice, the place where the soul is trying to go.

Your South Node in Gemini in the 12th house describes a soul pattern of multiplicity and mirroring in the unconscious layer — adaptability, linguistic intelligence, the ability to read rooms and reflect them back, the habit of dissolving into what's expected rather than holding a fixed position. The shadow of this South Node is using the adaptability to avoid the full-weight commitment the North Node in Sagittarius (6th house) requires.

Your North Node in Sagittarius in the 6th house is the direction: expansion, teaching, philosophical depth, the daily work as the arena of soul development. Not just having ideas but making them the substance of what you do every day. The 6th house is the house of practice — the node here says the soul grows through the work itself, not despite it.

Uranus at 2.60° Gemini is now opposing the NNode and conjuncting the SNode simultaneously. This is a nodal axis transit — it activates both ends of the soul trajectory at once.

What Uranus conjunct SNode does: it disrupts the comfort of the old pattern. The Gemini South Node pattern — adaptability, mirroring, multiplicity — gets a Uranian jolt. Whatever version of the South Node habit is still operating gets interrupted. Suddenly. Sometimes uncomfortably. The disruption is not punishment — it is the old groove becoming less comfortable than the new direction.

What Uranus opposing NNode does: it introduces sudden information, redirections, or structural breaks into the soul growth path itself. The 6th house Sagittarius work — the daily practice, the expansion through depth, the teaching — gets an unexpected input from the Gemini register. Not a wrong turn but a sudden course correction that will make more sense in retrospect than it does in the moment it arrives.

Together: the soul direction is being actively reconfigured. The old pattern gets disrupted (SNode conj Uranus). The new direction gets new information (NNode opp Uranus). The result is a period that can feel structurally uncertain — things that seemed settled in the life direction may pivot — but is actually moving the soul trajectory more definitively toward where it is meant to go.

The conjunction of Uranus with the South Node in the 12th house is particularly significant for the unconscious layer. 12th house material operates as atmosphere, as default responses, as the thing you notice only after the fact. Uranus transiting the 12th house SNode is a below-the-waterline disruption of habitual soul patterns. Dreams may be more vivid. Unconscious material may surface unexpectedly. The automatic pilot may fail in ways that reveal what was running invisibly. This is useful, not destabilizing.
