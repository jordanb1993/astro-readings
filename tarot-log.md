**Context:** Post-Tony week processing. Release ritual performed with sage, salt water, bay leaf burning, cord cutting, black and white candles.

| Clarifying | Ace of Scythes | Clarity available — the sword of truth, clean cut |

**Ritual notes:** Space cleared with sage, salt water at forehead/throat/heart/hands, bay leaves with written releases burned, cord cutting, black candle (absorbing) + white candle (identity/clarity), rosemary (memory of power) + lavender (settling).
