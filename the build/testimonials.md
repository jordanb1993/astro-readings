## What to Capture

When adding an entry, note:
- Who (first name + context is enough — no need for full names in every case)
- When
- What they said, as close to exact as possible
- What product/format/piece of work prompted it
- One-line takeaway: what does this confirm about what's working

This file feeds directly into Etsy listing copy, the business pitch, and eventually the app's social proof layer. Every reaction is data.
