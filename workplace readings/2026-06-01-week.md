## For Communication

Mercury in Gemini was information-optimized: fast, precise, high-volume. Cancer Mercury isn't slow — it's selective. It asks what matters before it speaks. In the workplace that means one-on-ones tend to be more candid than group meetings, personal connection opens doors that logic alone wouldn't, and people share more of what's actually true rather than what's on the agenda.

The caveat: Mercury immediately squares Neptune in Aries as it enters Cancer, with the square peaking Tuesday through Wednesday. That combination produces inspired communication that skips the evidence step — the instinct feels right, but the underlying assumptions haven't been checked. Write it Tuesday. Send it Wednesday after the Sun-Saturn structure kicks in.

The Mercury-Chiron sextile is exact today (both at 29° of their signs, the last degree, a finishing energy). This aspect favors articulating something that's been hard to say — the kind of communication that closes a loop rather than opens a new one. Use it.

**Best days for communication:** Wednesday–Thursday (Sun-Saturn grounding). **Watch:** Tuesday — inspired but potentially unexamined. **Good for:** Direct personal conversations, one-on-ones, anything where warmth carries more weight than precision.
