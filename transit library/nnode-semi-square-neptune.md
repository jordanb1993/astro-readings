## What This Is

The transiting North Node is currently at 2.71° Pisces, moving retrograde through the zodiac at approximately 0.05°/day. It forms a semi-square (45°) to natal Neptune at ~19.71° Capricorn in the 7th house.

The semi-square is not a dramatic aspect. Not the confrontational pressure of a square, not the active disruption of an opposition. It's a 45° angle: a persistent, low-frequency friction between two chart points that are neither in natural flow nor in direct tension. The signal is more like a stone in a shoe than a closed door. Navigable, but impossible to ignore entirely once you notice it.

Your natal Neptune in Capricorn in the 7th is the chart signature of idealization in partnership and collaboration. Neptune here doesn't create casual relationships. It creates ones that carry a quality of vision or transcendence, for better or worse. The 7th house is where you meet others in close 1:1 contact: lovers, collaborators, business partners, the people whose work runs alongside yours. Capricorn grounds Neptune's dissolution in structure and ambition; the relational idealism tends to organize around real-world building rather than pure fantasy. But the idealization is still Neptune's doing. The gap between what a partnership feels like it could become and what it actually is can run wide without Capricorn's scaffolding to close it.

The transiting North Node in Pisces represents the current direction of growth: the Piscean evolutionary edge of surrender, dissolution, trust in the invisible layer. When this Piscean Node forms a semi-square to the natal Capricorn Neptune, it's creating a minor friction between two dissolving forces. The soul direction's call toward Piscean depth, and the natal pattern of relational idealization that already lives in the 7th house. The friction is about calibration: when the soul is moving toward more dissolution (Pisces NNode), the existing Neptune patterns in the relational field tend to amplify. The signal is to notice which Neptune-flavored relational dynamics are getting louder right now, the inspired ones and the idealized ones alike.

Because this is a fast transit (the Node moves quickly and passes through orb in weeks), the friction is brief. Not a major relational reckoning. A course-check: a minor adjustment signal in the relational field as the soul's Piscean growth direction presses on it.
