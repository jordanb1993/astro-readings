There's a settling quality to today that doesn't announce itself. The Uranus-Jupiter inconjunct has been in the system since March, pressing from an angle that doesn't line up cleanly with anything else in the chart. An adjustment that couldn't be hurried, couldn't be worked around, that required the distance to actually move from 5° orb all the way to contact. Today it arrives at 0.04°. The pressure doesn't resolve into relief; it resolves into a click, the way something finally sits right after months of being slightly canted.

The inconjunct is the 150° aspect: not challenge, not flow, but persistent friction between two parts of the chart that can't see each other directly. Uranus in Gemini traffics in quick reframes, multiple simultaneous framings, the pivot that arrives sideways from an unexpected direction. Natal Jupiter in Scorpio in the 5th works at depth or not at all. Jupiter here doesn't do preview versions. The friction between these two registers has been specific: the work operates at Scorpio depth, and the language available to describe or deliver that work has been running in a mismatched register. Gemini wants to lead with the accessible entry point. Scorpio Jupiter says the depth is the entry point. The inconjunct says: find the translation layer, or keep grinding against the gap. Today the translation layer is found. What that looks like concretely is less a single event and more a shift in how the framing holds.

Venus is at 1°08' Leo this morning, having crossed from Cancer overnight. Yesterday's question at the anaretic Cancer degree was about desire and whether it needs protection. Leo doesn't ask that. Leo states what it wants in plain sight. For the 5th house territory, where everything either receives the full creative and relational investment or doesn't receive it at all, Venus in Leo adds a different operating frequency: desire becomes visible, claims its form, doesn't wait for conditions to be right before presenting itself. The trine Venus is forming to natal Sun in the 6th (0.73° applying) keeps this in a coherent forward motion through the week.

Mars in Taurus just passed exact trine to natal Uranus in the 7th at 0.03° separating. The 7th house carries natal Uranus-Neptune in Capricorn: the relational field has disruption and dissolution woven into its foundation. A Taurus Mars making a trine to that natal placement is a morning of ease through the relational channel, the unexpected that arrives as welcome rather than destabilizing. It moves on now. The nodal story activates next: NNode square natal Sun is at 0.58° applying, with exact contact June 15-17. Uranus opposition natal NNode tightens at 0.21°, exact June 17-18. The soul-direction axis and the work identity are being pressed from both ends simultaneously next week. What today's inconjunct resolves in the framing register is what that pressure arrives into.
