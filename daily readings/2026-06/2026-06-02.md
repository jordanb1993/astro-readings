## 🩹 Chiron at 29°: The Wound Meets the Threshold

Transiting Chiron at 29°16' Aries is applying to sextile your natal Ascendant at 29°22' Gemini — 0.11° from exact, the closest it gets this passage. What makes today unusual is the symmetry: both degrees are anaretic. Chiron is at the final degree of Aries, integrating something about assertion and the right to exist before it moves on. Your Ascendant sits at 29° Gemini — the threshold degree of your entire chart architecture, the face you show the world, and by conjunction, the South Node's home sign. Two completion degrees in productive dialogue. The sextile offers opportunity, not demand: the healing can flow into how you present yourself, hold your presence, move through the early hours of this workweek.

Your natal Chiron in Virgo in the 4th house carries the original wound pattern — the body must be useful, the self must earn its place, the inner home is never quite safe enough. What transiting Chiron in Aries brings is the opposite current: assertion, fire, the unqualified right to be. That current is sextiling the door — your Ascendant — today. You don't have to force anything. But if you notice the old Gemini-Ascendant contraction rising (the reflexive hedging, the softening of the thing you were about to say), today is an unusually good day to let something through that you'd normally hold back.

> *Two threshold degrees touching — the wound is not gone, but today it softens the door.*
